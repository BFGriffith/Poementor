WELCOME to “Poementor”

“Poementor” is for aspiring poets and lyricists: to streamline composing, editing, annotating, storing, and sharing all sorts of verse — as well as provide dynamic templating, modeling, and tutorial functionalities that conveniently aid writers in more easily conforming to various traditional poetical forms, metrical prosody structures, rhyme-schemes, etc.
