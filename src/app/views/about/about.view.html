about Poementor: ⎘Want to write a poem?🖋


Why “Poementor”?

“Poementor” is a “M.E.A.N.-stack” (Mongo, Express, Angular, Node) application for aspiring poets and lyricists that streamlines composing, editing, annotating, storing, and sharing all sorts of verse — as well as providing dynamic templating, modeling, and tutorial functionalities that conveniently aid writers in more easily conforming to various traditional poetical forms, metrical prosody structures, rhyme-schemes, etc.
